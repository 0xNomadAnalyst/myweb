## Slide 6 — System-Level Visibility
**Cross-Protocol Risk Becomes System-Level**

As stablecoins and yield-bearing RWAs span multiple DeFi services, risk is no longer local to any single venue.

- **Fragmented interfaces create partial views**  
  Official UIs and third-party tools rarely provide one coherent operating picture.

- **Metrics must be normalized across protocols**  
  Comparable definitions and consistent measurement are required to understand aggregate risk.

- **One system-level view is needed**  
  Teams need to track how liquidity, leverage, and execution conditions interact across venues.

- **Coverage can expand with the system**  
  Monitoring should evolve as new protocols become operationally relevant.


## Slide 7 — Premium Workflow
**Incident Replay & Root-Cause Analysis**

A historical event store turns stress events into measurable lessons — reducing time-to-understanding and accelerating policy refinement.

- **Historical event store**  
  Retain relevant events across covered protocols to support fast investigation when something breaks.

- **Rapid ad hoc diagnostics**  
  Shorten the path from “something happened” to “we understand why”.

- **Root-cause analysis**  
  Connect liquidity, execution conditions, and protocol exposures to identify mechanisms — not just correlations.

- **Accelerate the learning loop**  
  Refine operational playbooks, policies, and thresholds based on observed outcomes.

**Outcome:** faster recovery • fewer repeats • better policies
